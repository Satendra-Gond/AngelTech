What is e-RFQ (Electronic Request for Quotation)?

An e-RFQ (Electronic Request for Quotation) is a digital procurement solution that helps businesses create, send, receive, and compare supplier quotations through a centralized online platform.

It replaces traditional manual RFQ processes involving emails, spreadsheets, and paperwork with an intelligent and automated workflow that simplifies supplier communication and quotation management.

The platform enables procurement teams to accelerate approvals, improve quotation accuracy, reduce sourcing cycle times, and make faster and more informed purchasing decisions.

AngelTech Logistics' AI-powered e-RFQ Software simplifies the entire sourcing process with intelligent automation, real-time visibility, efficient vendor collaboration, and data-driven procurement insights.

Frequently Asked Questions

e-RFQ Software FAQs

1. What is e-RFQ (Electronic Request for Quotation)?

An e-RFQ is a digital procurement process that allows organizations to create, distribute, receive, evaluate, and manage supplier quotations through a centralized online platform.

2. How does e-RFQ software improve procurement?

e-RFQ software automates RFQ creation, supplier communication, quotation comparison, approval workflows, and reporting, helping businesses reduce procurement cycle times and improve purchasing decisions.

3. Who can use e-RFQ software?

Manufacturers, logistics companies, retailers, distributors, pharmaceutical companies, construction firms, engineering businesses, healthcare organizations, and enterprises with structured procurement processes can benefit from e-RFQ software.

4. Can suppliers submit quotations online?

Yes. Suppliers can securely access the vendor portal, respond to RFQs, upload supporting documents, and track quotation status online from a self-service interface.

5. Does the software support approval workflows?

Yes. Multi-level approval workflows can be configured based on departments, purchase values, business units, approval hierarchies, and organizational procurement policies.

6. Can the platform integrate with ERP systems?

Yes. AngelTech Logistics supports integration with SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, ERPNext, Odoo, TallyPrime, and other enterprise applications through APIs and integration services.

7. Is the software suitable for multi-location organizations?

Yes. The platform is designed to support multiple business units, procurement teams, warehouses, manufacturing plants, branch offices, and supplier networks from a centralized environment.

8. Is the software cloud-based and secure?

Yes. AngelTech Logistics is a secure cloud-based SaaS procurement platform with role-based access control, data encryption, audit trails, and enterprise-grade security standards.
