AI Logistics Software Solutions Built for Every Stage of Your Supply Chain

e-RFQ (Electronic Request for Quotation)

Accelerate freight procurement with an AI-powered e-RFQ platform that automates quotation requests, carrier responses, and bid evaluation. Compare multiple quotes in real time, select the best transportation partner, and reduce procurement time while optimizing logistics costs.

  • Create and distribute RFQs instantly
  • Receive and compare carrier quotations
  • AI-powered bid evaluation and recommendations
  • Faster procurement with complete audit history

Freight Audit, Verification & Reporting (FVR)

Eliminate billing errors and simplify freight invoice processing with AI-powered verification. Automatically validate invoices against contracts, shipment records, and agreed rates to improve financial accuracy, reduce disputes, and accelerate payment approvals.

  • AI-powered freight invoice verification
  • Automated contract and tariff validation
  • Detect overcharges and billing discrepancies
  • Comprehensive audit reports and compliance tracking

Transport Management System (TMS)

Optimize transportation planning, execution, and delivery with an AI-powered Transport Management System. Automate shipment scheduling, carrier selection, route optimization, and real-time tracking to reduce logistics costs while improving operational efficiency and on-time deliveries.

  • Automated carrier and shipment management
  • Real-time shipment tracking and visibility
  • Performance analytics and transportation reporting

Shipment Tracking

Track every shipment with complete visibility using an AI-powered Shipment Tracking solution. Monitor real-time locations, delivery milestones, estimated arrival times, and shipment exceptions to ensure timely deliveries, improve customer satisfaction, and maintain full control over your logistics operations.
