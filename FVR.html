What is Freight Software?

Freight Software is an AI-powered solution that automates freight invoice verification, shipment validation, freight auditing, contract compliance, and transportation reporting from a single centralized platform.

Using Artificial Intelligence (AI), OCR, and intelligent automation, it verifies freight invoices against contracts, shipment records, and supporting documents before payment approval.

By replacing manual processes with intelligent automation, Freight Software helps businesses reduce billing errors, prevent duplicate payments, improve compliance, accelerate approvals, and gain real-time visibility into freight costs, carrier performance, and logistics operations.

Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is Freight Verification Software?

Freight Verification Software is an intelligent solution that automates freight invoice verification, shipment validation, contract matching, freight auditing, and payment approvals before invoices are processed.

2. How does AI-powered Freight Verification work?

The software uses Artificial Intelligence, OCR, and business rules to automatically compare freight invoices with shipment records, contracts, carrier information, taxes, and supporting documents, ensuring accurate invoice verification.

3. What is Freight Audit Software?

Freight Audit Software automatically identifies duplicate invoices, billing discrepancies, incorrect freight charges, pricing errors, tax issues, and contract violations before payment approval.

4. How does OCR improve freight invoice processing?

OCR technology extracts invoice information from scanned documents, PDFs, emails, and digital invoices, eliminating manual data entry while improving processing speed and accuracy.

5. Can the software integrate with ERP systems?

Yes. The platform integrates with leading ERP systems such as SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, Tally, and custom enterprise applications for automated invoice posting and financial reconciliation.

6. Does the software support Transportation Management Systems (TMS)?

Yes. It integrates seamlessly with TMS platforms to synchronize shipment data, carrier information, and transportation records.

7. Can the software detect duplicate freight invoices?

Yes. AI-powered validation automatically identifies duplicate invoices before payment approval, helping prevent duplicate payments and revenue leakage.

8. How does the platform validate freight charges?

The software compares invoice charges with approved freight contracts, shipment details, negotiated rates, business rules, taxes, and carrier agreements.
